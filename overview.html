How it Works

HomeFood connects people interested in consuming home-cooked food, and people who are able to provide a home-cooked meal. The food givers post meals that they are preparing, a suggested donation for the meal, and the number of servings that they can provide to food seekers. Then food seekers are able to navigate the website of food in their local area and select the food that they are interested in. Then the food seekers go pick up the food, and pay some form of donation to the food givers, making it so any person capable of cooking can generate some level of disposable income.
